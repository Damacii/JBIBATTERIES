About JBI Batteries

Power solutions you can count on.

At JBI Batteries, we believe power should be reliable, straightforward, and built around real-world needs. For decades, we’ve helped drivers, fleets, and businesses find the right battery solutions—without guesswork.

Based in Hesperia and Fontana, California, JBI Batteries serves a wide range of customers, from individual vehicle owners to commercial operations that depend on uptime every day. We carry trusted battery brands and support applications across automotive, marine, commercial trucks, and powersports.

What sets us apart isn’t just what we sell—it’s how we support our customers. We focus on practical guidance, honest recommendations, and dependable service. Whether you’re replacing a single battery or managing inventory for an entire fleet, our team works to make the process efficient and stress-free.

We also take responsibility seriously. JBI Batteries supports proper battery recycling and works to ensure every product we sell is backed by clear warranty support and real accountability.

At the end of the day, our goal is simple: deliver power solutions you can count on, backed by people who know what they’re doing.
